Emergency Helpline

If you have found a missing child or need urgent help, please reach out to our support team immediately. We are available 24/7 to assist families and coordinate with authorities.

Contact Details

  • Emergency Helpline: +1 800-123-4567
  • Email: support@lostandfound.org
  • Live Chat: Available 24/7 on our website
  • Local Authorities: Contact your nearest police station

How to Report?

If you need to report a missing child, please provide as many details as possible, including a recent photo, last known location, and any identifying features. Your report will be reviewed and shared with relevant authorities.
