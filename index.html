About Lost & Found

Losing a child in a crowded place can be terrifying. Our platform provides a way for parents and guardians to post details of missing children, enabling volunteers and authorities to help reunite families.

How it Works:

  • Parents/Guardians can report missing children.
  • Volunteers and authorities can browse reports and help locate lost kids.
  • Secure and verified communication between concerned individuals.

Why Choose Us?

Our platform is designed for quick action, allowing real-time updates and easy reporting. We work with local authorities to ensure a safe and efficient recovery process.
